End-to-End Flow — “Run an evaluation”

4 lanes, 1 audit chain, 4 Claude calls

Click & dispatch

React fires POST /api/v1/evaluations/run. Axios attaches the JWT; FastAPI middleware verifies it, re-fetches the user’s role, enforces maintainer+, and emits eval_run_started to the audit ledger.

Load & gate

eval_runner.py loads EvalTestCase rows for the service. The sensitivity gate fires: confidential services require an explicit admin allow_confidential=true override, itself audited.

Actor → Judges

Per test case: Sonnet 4.6 generates the answer through the 6-stage pipeline. Then Haiku 4.5 runs score_factuality and detect_hallucination. Each call books its own cost at the correct per-model rate.

Aggregate & alert

EvalRun persists with judge_model stamped. Split-half drift math runs; if the recent window is >15 points below baseline, an Alert row is created with severity Critical and drift_detected is appended to the chain.

Cross-Cutting Concerns

Applied at every layer · enforced by construction

Auth

JWT at middleware. bcrypt in auth.py. LoginAttempt throttles after 5 failures per 15 min.

RBAC

Depends(require_role) per route. Roles re-fetched every request. 403s audited as role_denied.

Audit

middleware/audit.py — SHA-256 hash chain under threading.Lock. SQLite triggers block app-path UPDATE/DELETE.

SSRF

url_validator.py runs at 4 points: register, update, test probe, scheduled tick. DNS rebinding defeated.

Safety

15 regex tripwires + independent Haiku classifier. Fail-open. Risk = max(regex, classifier).

Budget

Atomic check-and-reserve under _BUDGET_LOCK. $5 / day, $25 / month caps. Sized for 4 calls/request.

HITL

AILlmDraft + draft_service.py. 20-char reviewer note at 3 layers. Idempotent approval.

Drift

Split-half: recent window vs. historical baseline. Absolute floor at Quality < 75. Strict judge parser excludes refusals.
